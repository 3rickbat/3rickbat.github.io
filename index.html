Master's Thesis Scholarly Article
A critical look at patching processes in the video game League of Legends. Looks at patches as a cultural artifact that can be examined. Ties in practices of continuously updating a live service game to concepts such as labor, race, and technology. Written as a graduation requirement for the University of Texas at Dallas.
